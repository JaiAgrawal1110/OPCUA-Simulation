What is MachBridge?

MachBridge is a revolutionary platform that connects CNC machines to a digital ecosystem using OPC UA protocols, unlocking the power of real-time data and AI-driven insights.

Whether you're looking to enhance operational efficiency, monitor machine health, or implement predictive maintenance strategies — MachBridge does it all with precision and scalability.

Core Features

  • Real-time CNC Data Acquisition – Stream and store vital machine data effortlessly.
  • OPC UA Integration – Industry-standard secure communication with machines.
  • AI & ML Analytics – From anomaly detection to performance forecasting.
  • Custom Dashboards – Visualize insights that matter most to your operations.
  • Scalable Architecture – From small setups to industrial-scale deployments.
